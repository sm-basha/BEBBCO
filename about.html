About Us

B E B B C O

We have started with the establishment of BHARAT ENGG. & BODY BUILDING CO. PVT. LTD. in 1983 and after that we have excelled under the leadership of our Chairman Shree Gajanand Bhalotia.

Now, BEBBCO (BHARAT Group) is active in diversified businesses, and in the meantime, we have also become synonyms for the fabrication of automobile bodies in the region.

At BEBBCO we build customized bodies for automobiles depending on the purpose of the vehicle and the needs of the customer. We fabricate the automobile bodies on the top of TATA, EICHER, Ashok Leyland, BHARAT BENZ, URAL chassis ranging from 2600 WB to 6200 WB, for domestic as well as for the export market.

All the processes involved in the manufacturing i.e. right from the designing to development of the final product are done under a single roof at our Ultra Modern manufacturing plant in Jamshedpur.

As we are involved in making both the passenger as well as industrial commercial vehicles, we are dedicated to ensuring a safe, secure and comfortable journey to its passengers and on the other hand, our tendency of using high-quality materials and delivering them at competitive rates brings optimum benefits to our commercial clients. ents.

We are constantly making efforts to become one of the best in the industry while taking care of the needs of our customers, employees, and society. We are also thriving to set new benchmarks with our quality, timely delivery, and teamwork.

Message from our Chairman

"Coming together is the beginning. Keeping together is progress. Working together is success."     - Henry Ford

From the very first day to the present day, I have always tried to go by this favorite quote of mine because I have always believed that the most pleasing way to enjoy success is to share it with the people who made it possible through their dedication and teamwork.

Years ago, It all started with the growing desire to become one of the pioneers in the Vehicle Body Building Industry in the region. Thus, the BHARAT Group was born in 1983. We have started with the establishment of BHARAT ENGG. & BODY BUILDING CO. PVT. LTD., our first manufacturing plant in Jamshedpur. In the beginning, we have tried to fulfill the growing demand for Buses, Load Bodies, Defense Vehicles, Tippers and Tip-Trailers, and other industrial commercial vehicles. In the upcoming years with the growth in the automobile industry in India, our growth also has been exponential and we have expanded our business to fabricating Luxury Buses, Ambulances, Containers, Motorhomes, Special Purpose Vehicles, and many more.

Over the years, our customer base has extended not only in multiple states of India but also in we became a well-recognized brand in the foreign markets of the Middle East and South African Countries.

Our core value lies in the happiness of our employees and appreciation from our customers while our core strength is after-sales support and services. These ethics have helped us to build good relationships with our customers.

We have always believed that our employees are the most important asset to our company and we leave no stones unturned to nurture their careers while counting on their skills and potential. Our goal is to create a healthy work atmosphere by bringing in transparency and treating everyone equally and to give our employees ample opportunities to grow and prosper while keeping an eye on protecting their health and safety.

Our future plan is simple, we are expanding and we will continue to do so by adding new business partners and improving our services. For the last 3 decades, we have strived to set new benchmarks for us and we will carry on this approach with our quality and innovation.

At last, I can proudly say that I am honored and privileged to be the Chairman and Managing Director of the BHARAT GROUP. I also want to thank all who are on the journey of ultimately achieving the shared vision of becoming the best in the industry and getting global recognition.

Gajanand Bhalotia

Chairman & Managing Director
