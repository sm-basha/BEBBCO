About BEBBCO

Railway & Defence Vehicle Fabrications
Special Fabrications
Emergency vehicle Fabrications
Luxury coaches Fabrications

With a legacy of over 35+ Years, BEBBCO is active in Body Building of Buses, Tippers, Load Bodies, Defence Vehicles, Ambulances, Motorhomes, Specialized Vehicles, Containers and much more.

Established in 1983, BEBBCO is now a renowned brand name not only in Jharkhand but also in other states like Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, West Bengal, Assam, Rajasthan, and Delhi. In the meantime, we have also become an acclaimed exporter for foreign countries like the United Aram Emirates, Qatar, South Africa, Ghana, and Mozambique.

We are constantly making our mark in the vehicle Body Building industry and we are proud to say that we have built over 15000 units of bus body and over 30000 units of Army load bodies on the top of TATA, Ashok Leyland, Mahindra, BharatBenz, Ural, Eicher chassis.
